MARIA CRAWFORD

is a singer-songwriter from Philadelphia. Maria’s music conveys a vulnerability that makes her songs of hope, regret, and grace an intensely personal experience. Her lyrics speak of redemption with the intimacy of experience, evoking genuine nostalgia and emotion.

Hi there!

My name is Maria but my friends call me “Mar” so let’s be friends!

I hail from the great city of Philadelphia and now live with my best friends and our four chickens in Nashville, TN. I grew up playing piano and writing songs in my bedroom in efforts to not feel so alone. Over time I discovered how much music meant to me and that the purpose of sharing my songs goes far beyond myself. I have found that the music I create helps me understand a much greater story than my own. A story that speaks of grace and joy, where those who think they are not are made into something more.

I am excited to share with you these songs of mine that come from a very vulnerable place. A place of hurt, regret, and healing. It is my hope that through my attempt at honesty you can find a place of comfort where your humanness can relate to my humanness. It is my wish that you discover you’re not too far gone from saving, you’re not too much to handle, and you’re the farthest thing from alone.

Love mucho grande,

Mar
